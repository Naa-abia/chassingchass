Checklist

Preparation Checklist/Things to Note:

COVID Tests

Must take COVID test (PCR COVID test not rapid) on December 24th or 25th prior to leaving US (cost varies/some insurance covers)

Will need to take another COVID test when landing in Accra Airport (about $150 USD cash/credit card)

Will need to take another COVID test prior to leaving Ghana (about $65 USD cash only)

Vaccinated passengers will still need to take all COVID tests

Due to the new COVID variant that has been announced, the government of Ghana is advising all travelers into Ghana to be vaccinated. If you are vaccinated, please travel with your vaccination card and/or health waivers.

Please complete your Ghana Health Declaration Form on the following website:https://ghs-hdf.org/.

COVID tests need to be taken within 72 hours of departure. A Negative Covid test result needs to be shown at the airline before they will allow you to board the flight.

Payment for the COVID test that will be administered when you arrive at the airport in Ghana must be done online at Https://myfrontierhealthcare.com

Vaccinations/Shots

(Click here for details on CDC website)

• Required-Yellow Fever vaccination.
Click Here for list of clinics who administer the vaccine in US. MUST carry yellow fever card as it will be checked to clear passport control and customs. (NOTE: I would suggest making an appointment for this vaccine ASAP as clinics have been known to run out)

• High Recommended- Malaria pills.
You can visit a travel health clinic (CVS or Passport Health) or check with your normal physician for prescription. You can also check with the clinic you make appointment for Yellow Fever Vaccination to see if they can write prescription for Malaria pills

• Highly Recommended Now - Polio Vaccination
If you were vaccinated against polio as a child but have never had a polio booster dose as an adult, you should get this booster dose. Adults need only one polio booster dose in their lives. Check with the clinic you make appointment for Yellow Fever Vaccination to see if they can administer Polio or Polio booster vaccination OR normal physician

• There are other recommended vaccinations as listed on the Center of Disease Control.
These are not required, and you can consider getting them at your own discretion.

• Highly Recommended Now- COVID Vaccine

Important Items to Bring

Physical Passport and a Copy of Passport (Digital and Printed)

Yellow Fever Vaccination Card

COVID Vaccination Card (if applicable)

At least $800 USD cash (if this is within your budget to pay for meals not included in itinerary, souvenirs, tips/gratuities, activities not included in itinerary).

a. NOTE: PLEASE BRING ALL NEW /CRISP $100 BILLS

b. Credit card machines/ATM may have issues so always good to carry more than enough cash.

Credit Card and Debit Card (Ensure to contact bank and let them know you will be traveling)

School items for volunteer event (list will be provided at Meet and Greet)

Insect repellent (look for ingredients containing DEET, Picaridin Oil of lemon eucalyptus (OLE) OR IR3535)

Travel Adapter - Please purchase an universal adapter. Ghana uses type D and G outlets.

Wireless phone charger

Professional Camera/Quality phone camera (photographer will be there to capture moments but bring good camera in case you want more personal shots)

Sunscreen-30-50 SPF

Hand Sanitizer

Antacids tablets (Tums or Pepto bismol)

Contact mobile company regarding international data plan (WIFI will be scarce in Africa)

Summer Wear (Weather in Ghana will be 85 F or higher)

Swim Wear

Comfortable shoes (sandals and or gym shoes)

All White & All Black Outfit (Going Out)

An unlocked phone that can accept a sim card

1-2 Bottles of your favorite Alcohol (Liquor stores are hard to find and you can also get at Duty Free inairport. May be more expensive and limited options)

Small Carry-on bag or Book bag

And Most important Patience, Positivity and an Open-Mind!

Phone/Wifi

  • Wifi will be available in hotel and on tour bus to scheduled events
  • Contact mobile company regarding international data plan (WIFI will be scarce in Africa)
  • If phone coverage does not work, the following will be available for purchase on arrival:
    • a. Sim card with data plan- about $20. Please check with carrier to confirm phone is unlocked and can accept foreign sim cards. If not, you can bring an unlocked phone or purchase one in Accra
    • b. Mobile hotspot- about $60 (but can connect multiple devices so can share price with someone you will be with the entire trip
