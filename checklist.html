Notes Before Traveling to West Africa

Vaccinations Pills

1. Required-Yellow Fever vaccination.
Click Here for list of clinics who administer the vaccine in US. MUST carry yellow fever card as it will be checked to clear passport control and customs. (NOTE: I would suggest making an appointment for this vaccine ASAP as clinics have been known to run out)

2. High Recommended- Malaria pills.
You can visit a travel health clinic (CVS or Passport Health) or check with your normal physician for prescription. You can also check with the clinic you make appointment for Yellow Fever Vaccination to see if they can write prescription for Malaria pills

3. Highly Recommended Now - Polio Vaccination
If you were vaccinated against polio as a child but have never had a polio booster dose as an adult, you should get this booster dose. Adults need only one polio booster dose in their lives. Check with the clinic you make appointment for Yellow Fever Vaccination to see if they can administer Polio or Polio booster vaccination OR normal physician

4. There are other recommended vaccinations as listed on the Center of Disease Control.
These are not required, and you can consider getting them at your own discretion.

Important Items to Bring

1.Physical Passport & Copy of Passport (in case passport is lost/ stolen)

2.Visa- If you are based in US will need your passport by October 15th to apply for visa. You will get it back by mid Novemeber.

3. At least $750 USD Cash OR Equivalent in Ghana Cedis (Some places may not take credit card)

4. Credit Card and Debit Card (Ensure to contact bank and let them know you will be traveling)

5. Insect repellent (look for ingredients containing DEET, Picaridin Oil of lemon eucalyptus (OLE) ORIR3535)

6. Travel Adapter - Please purchase an universal adapter. Ghana uses type D and G outlets.

7. Sunscreen-30-50 SPF

8. Hand Sanitizer

9. Antacids tablets (Tums or Pepto bismol)

10. Contact mobile company regarding international data plan (WIFI will be scarce in Africa )

11. Summer Wear (Weather in Ghana will be 85 F or higher)

12. Swim Wear (There will be beach time and pool at hotel. Please bring at least one solid/neon colored swim suit for pictures)

13. Comfortable shoes (sandals and or gym shoes)

14. White Dress for Traditional Wedding Ceremony and to wear to gala ceremony (will send a separate email)

15. NYE outfit (All black)

16. 1-2 Bottles of your favorite Alcohol (Liquor stores are hard to find and you can also get at Duty Free inairport. May be more expensive and limited options
