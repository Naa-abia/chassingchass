Global Apprenticeship Scholarship

In line with our mission to create a culture of travel which promotes meaningful diversity and inclusion, we are sponsoring two youths of the African diaspora who reside in an underserved communities in the US and Caribbean and will extend them the opportunity to gain work experience in an Assistant Manager, Global Experiences apprenticeship. Join us and be a part of something that will change your life..

Job Description :

  • Share ideas and execute activities to drive attendee engagement and enhance overall experience
  • Capture moments on trip via photography/videography.
  • Create and manage marketing collaterals, website and social media content
  • Collaborate effectively with local team
  • Coordinate planning meetings, attendee touch base meetings, Meet and Greets, etc.

Assistant Manager, Global Experiences Apprenticeship

  • Share ideas and execute activities to drive attendee engagement and enhance overall experience
  • Capture moments on trip via photography/videography.
  • Create and manage marketing collaterals, website and social media content
  • Collaborate effectively with local team
  • Coordinate planning meetings, attendee touch base meetings, Meet and Greets, etc.
  • Responsible for creating post-trip survey and gathering attendee feedback for continuous improvement
  • Perform additional administrative and trip-related tasks as needed and directed
  • Assist tour guides and trip management to ensure a successful execution of trip
  • Conduct independent research to learn about culture/customs of country prior to arriving
  • Must be 21-25 years old
  • African American/ African Diaspora and reside in underserved community in US or US territory (Priority will be given to Maywood,IL residents)
  • Must have interest/passion marketing, photography, videography, website creation and/or social media management, customer experience, UX
  • Must be enrolled or graduated from a 2 or 4 year institution or trade program OR at least 5 years of direct marketing, photography, videography and/or social media management experience

Included in Apprenticeship Scholarship:

    - Passport(if applicant does not have one)
    • - Visa
      • - Round trip airfare
        • - Hotel Accommodations (double occupancy)
          • - Access to all tours and scheduled events
            • - Daily meals

What's not included

    - Cash allowance/salary
    • - Spending money for activities outside of schedule
      • - Required/Recommended Vaccinations and Pills
        • - Tips/Gratuities
