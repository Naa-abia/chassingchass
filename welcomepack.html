Welcome Pack

Know some Ghanaian words and Phrases (TWI)

Welcome -Akwaaba (ak-waa-ba)

  • Mema wo akwaaba (I welcome you) - (mih-Maa-waa-ak-waa-ba)
  • Yɛma wo akwaaba (We welcome you)- (yer-Maa-waa-ak-waa-ba)

Responses to Greetings

  • Yaa agya (reply - to an elderly man)
  • Yaa ɛna (reply - to an elderly woman)
  • Yaa anua (reply - to a sibling age mate or colleague

Greetings in Twi

  • Me ma wo akye - Good morning (mih-Maa-waa-chi)
  • Me ma wo aha - Good afternoon (mih-Maa-waa-ha)
  • Me ma wo adwo - Good evening (mih-Maa-waa-jo)
  • Da yie - Good night (dah-yey)
  • Nante yiye - Good Bye

Know some Phrases:

  • Medaase - Thank you (MIH-dah-sih)
  • Mepaa kyɛw - Please (meh-paw-chow)
  • Ɛte sεn? -How are you? ( er-te-SAIN)
  • mepɛ ... - I want (Me pɛ )
  • ɛyɛ sɛn? - How much is it ?

OTHER FACTS

If you do not want to eat with your hands then ask for utensils.

Greet elders of heads of family first.

Dress well: Ghanaians place a lot of emphasis on how people dress. You may need to remove your shoes
